Temple Etiquette

👘

Dress Code

Wear traditional or modest Indian attire. Avoid shorts, skirts, and revealing clothing inside temples.

🥿

Remove Shoes

Shoes must be removed before entering any temple premises. Shoe stands are available near entrances.

📸

Photography

Photography is prohibited inside the Jagannath Temple. Check rules at other temples before taking photos.
