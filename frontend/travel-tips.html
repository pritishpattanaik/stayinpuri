📍 Puri Travel Tips

Everything you need to know before visiting Puri — from the best time to visit to local customs and practical advice.

Getting to Puri

✈️

By Air

Nearest airport: Biju Patnaik International Airport (BBI), Bhubaneswar — 60 km from Puri. Taxis available at ₹1,800.

🚂

By Train

Puri is a terminal railway station with direct trains from Kolkata, Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, and Bangalore.

🚌

By Road

Well connected by NH316. 60 km from Bhubaneswar (1.5 hrs), 500 km from Kolkata (8 hrs). Regular buses available.

Best Time to Visit

Winter

Oct — Mar (Best)

Pleasant weather, 15-28°C. Ideal for sightseeing, temple visits, and beach activities.

Summer

Apr — Jun

Hot and humid, 25-40°C. Best time for early morning temple visits. Rath Yatra in June/July.

Monsoon

Jul — Sep

Heavy rainfall. Fewer tourists, lower prices. The sea is rough — swimming not recommended.

Rath Yatra

June/July

The grand chariot festival. Millions attend. Book months in advance. A once-in-a-lifetime experience.

What to Pack

👕 Clothing

Light cotton clothes in summer, warm layers in winter. Traditional Indian attire for temple visits. Comfortable walking shoes. Swimwear for the beach.

🧴 Essentials

Sunscreen, sunglasses, hat. Mosquito repellent. Basic medications. Umbrella in monsoon. Power bank. Reusable water bottle.

Local Customs & Etiquette

🙏 Respect the Culture

Puri is a deeply religious city. Be respectful at temples and religious ceremonies. Dress modestly near temple areas.

💱 Bargaining

Bargaining is common in local markets and with auto-rickshaws. Agree on prices before starting your ride or purchase.

🌊 Beach Safety

Swim only in designated areas. The sea can be rough. Avoid swimming during high tide and monsoon. Local lifeguards are present near main beach.

Day Trip Ideas

☀️ Konark Sun Temple

35 km / 1 hour

UNESCO World Heritage Site. Half-day trip. Combine with Chandrabhaga Beach.

🐦 Chilika Lake

50 km / 1.5 hours

Dolphin spotting, bird watching, Kalijai Temple island. Full-day trip with boat ride.

🏛️ Bhubaneswar

60 km / 1.5 hours

Lingaraj Temple, Udayagiri & Khandagiri caves, tribal museum. Full-day cultural tour.
